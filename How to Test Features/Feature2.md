### How to test Feature 2: Shortlist Job

To test this feature, once the app is launched, sign in to an account (sign up for an account if you dont have one). 

From there you will be taken to the home page. Here you should see no jobs shortlisted and it says "No shortlisted jobs found..." 

<img width="1470" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229634962-62cdb7a1-d0f1-4fbc-a589-73ce73ea59b4.png">

Now navigate to the filter page from the nav bar at the top. From there you will be taken the job filter page and see all the jobs in the databse. 

<img width="1461" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/228078596-ffcc601c-7e08-41c8-87f9-f7475663389a.png">

From here click on the "View" button next to any job which will take you to a detail page for that job. 

<img width="1470" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229635114-e4d37a2a-66f2-4eb0-b86c-63fab8336b40.png">

Now click on the "Shortlist" Button on the top right, which should then be highlighted once clicked and say "Shortlisted" 

<img width="1470" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229635219-a6012c7c-5c31-42d6-bb71-0e2d2cb1cb90.png">

Now navigate to "Home" using the top nav bar. Here you should see the job you just shortlisted. 

<img width="1470" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229635382-2130e5de-1a3f-4908-a24d-d4aa3ed5d6b0.png">

If you click "View" it should take you back to the job detail page. If you click "Remove" it should remove the job from the Shortlist as seen here: 
<img width="1470" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229635515-6b2d484c-4228-4bf2-b89a-2e303952bde1.png">

You can go on multiple jobs and shortlist them to have more than one job shortlisted whihc would like this: 

<img width="1468" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/30390967/229635665-9b7034ab-9727-4786-a999-aa3c59a0053e.png">

To verify this, you can see the results in the Shortlist table, where it maps the user ID to the job_posting_id of the jobs you have shortlisted.
